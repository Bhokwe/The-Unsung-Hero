# THE UNSUNG HERO
**Module:** Game Design 3A (GADS7331)  
**Developer:** Latita
**Platform:** PC (Python/Pygame)

---

## Project Overview
"The Unsung Hero" is an action-based reverse escort mission where the player takes on the role of a floating support sprite. Your job is to keep a reckless, AI-controlled "Hero" Knight alive as he charges head-first into danger, emphasizing the theme of being an unappreciated helper.

### Core Gameplay Loop
* **Support:** Facilitate the Knight’s survival despite his chaotic decision-making and hunger for chaos.
* **Manage:** Dash across the battlefield to collect "Life Orbs" dropped by enemies to heal the Knight.
* **Protect:** Use a "Pulse" ability to knock back enemies at a cost to the player's own health (Life Orbs).

---

## AI Integration & Tools
This project was developed during a three-day solo game jam, utilizing AI as a primary creative and technical collaborator.
* **Coding:** **Cursor** was used for rapid prototyping, developing the Knight's "Reckless" state machine, and debugging.
* **Art:** High-contrast 3D-style assets were generated via **Leonardo AI**.
* **Sound:** Triumphant "heroic" tracks and frantic player SFX were composed using **MusicGPT**.
* **Context Management:** Custom `.cursorrules`, `plan.md`, and `readme.md` files were used to guide AI outputs and maintain design consistency.
